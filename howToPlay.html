How to Play!

1. Click on the button to select the level of quiz.
2. Then click on Start Quiz button to start the quiz.
3. Read the rules of quiz carefully and then continue.
4. After completing the quiz you will get your score.
5. If you want to try to get a better score, click the Replay Quiz button at the bottom of the results page. You can try the quiz as many times as you like.
6. Do not try to switch the tab otherwise you will get warning.
